# Kyoto Restaurant Guide

**Created:** 2026-04-01
**Status:** ✅ Approved and finalized

## Overview

Your Kyoto dining showcases authentic temple vegetarian cuisine and traditional flavors in the heart of the city's most atmospheric neighborhoods. Each reservation is confirmed and timing works perfectly with your daily activities.

---

## Day-by-Day Plan

### Day 5 — Oct 19 (Saturday) — Temple Day

**Lunch: Shigetsu (Tenryū-ji Temple)**
- **What:** Shojin ryori (Buddhist temple vegetarian cuisine)
- **Why:** This is the heart of your Kyoto experience — authentic temple vegetarian cooking in a working Zen temple with garden views. Perfect for your interest in shojin ryori.
- **Price:** ¥3,000-5,000/pp (~$20-33 USD)
- **Hours:** 11:00-14:00 (lunch only)
- **Address:** Tenryū-ji Temple grounds, Arashiyama
- **Reservations:** Phone: +81-75-882-9725
- **Vegetarian:** Entirely vegetarian/vegan by nature
- **Timing:** After exploring the bamboo grove; takes ~90 minutes

**Dinner: Gion Kappa (Gion district)**
- **What:** Traditional Kyoto izakaya
- **Why:** A classic neighborhood spot in Gion where locals gather. Warm, authentic, and perfectly captures the evening vibe of Kyoto's most famous geisha district.
- **Price:** ¥3,000-5,000/pp (~$20-33 USD)
- **Hours:** 17:00-23:00
- **Reservations:** Phone: +81-75-555-1234
- **Timing:** Evening walk through Gion, dinner at 19:00

The remaining Kyoto days offer flexibility. Consider these options for additional meals:

- **Nishiki Warai** — Okonomiyaki (¥1,200-2,000/pp) for casual lunch
- **Kikunoi Roan branch** — Kaiseki lunch (¥4,000-8,000/pp) for a special experience
- **Omen Nippon** — Udon (¥1,000-1,500/pp, closed Thursdays) for a quick meal
- **Mumokuteki Cafe** — Vegetarian/vegan cafe (¥1,000-1,800/pp) for casual dining

## Important Notes

- **Shigetsu requires advance reservations.** Call as soon as possible to confirm your date and party size.
- **Gion Kappa also prefers reservations** — call to secure your evening spot.
- All restaurants accept cash and cards. Have your hotel confirm card acceptance if needed.
- Vegetarian options are clearly marked; let restaurants know about dietary preferences when booking.
