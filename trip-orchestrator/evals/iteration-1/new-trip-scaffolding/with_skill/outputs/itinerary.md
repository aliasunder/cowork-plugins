## Cities Under Consideration

Route will be finalized in Phase 2. Current thinking: 2-3 cities with 3+ nights each. Possible cities:
- **Tokyo:** Modern Japan, food scene, temples, gardens. Major hub. Risk: overwhelming, too much for "depth" trip if added to other cities.
- **Kyoto:** Traditional Japan, temples, gardens, cultural heart. Essential if temple/garden focused.
- **Osaka:** Food hub, more casual/accessible than Kyoto. Nearby Kyoto via train.
- **Hiroshima:** Peace Memorial, food, cultural significance. Different flavor from others.
- **Kanazawa:** Geisha district, Kenroku-en Garden, castle town, less touristy than Kyoto.
- **Naoshima:** Art island, gardens, unique cultural experience.
