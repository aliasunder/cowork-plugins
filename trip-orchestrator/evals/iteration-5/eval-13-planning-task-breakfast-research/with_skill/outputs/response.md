## The Three Spots — Details & Why I Picked Them

### 1. **Chez Lisette** — Best for Pastries & Local Character

**Location:** Place des Châtaignes (quiet square off main streets, intra-muros)
**Specialty:** Sacristan pastry (Avignon specialty — crispy, powdered sugar, slivered almonds), chocolate croissants, fougasse
**Coffee:** Good quality
**Vegetarian:** All pastries naturally vegetarian
**Atmosphere:** Local, quiet square, warm staff
**Price estimate:** €8–15/person
**Best for:** **May 27 morning** — before the Palais visit

**Why mom will love this:** The sacristan is a local Avignon pastry you can't get anywhere else. It's what locals eat for breakfast. The staff is known for being genuinely warm and welcoming, not rushed. It's on a quiet square — the kind of spot you find by accident and feel special having discovered.

**Practical note:** May need to confirm hours and phone ahead; might have queues on peak mornings (call hotel for tip on best time to go).

---

### 2. **Café Tulipe** — Best for Coffee + Vegetarian Options

**Location:** ~7 min walk from main square (intra-muros area)
**Specialty:** Exceptional coffee (La Marzocco machine, locally sourced), locally made cakes
**Vegetarian/Vegan:** Excellent — vegan toast, avocado toast, tofu veggie bowl, carrot cake, local organic options
**Atmosphere:** Cosy with comfy couches, shares space with a yoga studio and ethical shop — relaxed, quality-focused vibe
**Price estimate:** €10–18/person
**Best for:** **May 29 morning (last day)** or if sleeping in and wanting a leisurely, high-quality option

**Why this works for both of you:** Your mom will appreciate the quality and character. You'll appreciate the explicitly strong vegetarian options and ethical sourcing. The coffee is genuinely excellent (reviewers come back for the coffee alone). The couches invite you to linger.

**Practical note:** Well-reviewed across multiple platforms, should have reliable hours. Worth a visit on your final morning.

---

### 3. **Le Saint Chocolat** — Best Value + Backup

**Location:** Place Saint Didier (confirmed intra-muros, likely close to hotel)
**Breakfast deal:** €2.30 for a croissant aux amandes + organic coffee — unbeatable
**Pastries:** Homemade croissants, tarts, macarons, viennoiseries
**Vegetarian:** Viennoiseries naturally veg-friendly; salads available for something more substantial
**Atmosphere:** Cosy self-service café, central, no-fuss
**Price estimate:** €3–12/person depending on what you order
**Best for:** **May 28 morning (day-trip day)** — quick, reliable, great value

**Why this is the practical choice:** You have an early 8:30 À La Française pickup on May 28. You'll want breakfast fast but good. Le Saint Chocolat's €2.30 deal gets you out the door happy and caffeinated. It's also a solid backup for any other morning if Lisette has queues.
