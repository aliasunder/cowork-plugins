## Key Constraints & How They're Addressed

### 1. You're Vegetarian
- **Research approach:** Don't lead with "vegetarian options." Present vegetarian & omnivore dishes equally.
- **Focus:** Buddhist temple cuisine (shojin ryori), vegetable-forward ramen, sushi, markets, convenience stores.
- **Day-by-day:** Slot restaurants showing both options side-by-side (not segregated).

### 2. Alex's Knee (Stairs Painful After Long Days)
- **Activity design:** 1 main per day, mid-day breaks (1–2pm rest in hotel/café), no all-day hikes.
- **Accommodation:** Elevator or low-stair entry, ground/1st floor preferred.
- **Transport:** Taxis for tired evenings (no forcing metro after exhausting day).
- **Guides:** Note stairs/elevators factually, but don't over-medicalize in shared materials.
- **Accessibility:** Research every site beforehand (stairs, resting spots, accessibility routes).

### 3. Language Anxiety (Both)
- **Phrasebook:** Japanese phrases by situation (restaurant, hotel, transit, "I'm vegetarian," "Are there stairs?", emergencies).
- **Tech:** Translation app (Google Translate, offline mode) pre-loaded, camera translation feature.
- **Confidence-building:** Consider 1 guided activity Day 1 (walking tour, temple guide). Hotel staff usually English-competent in 3–4 star hotels.
- **Photo strategy:** Carry photos of hotel, phone numbers, use camera to point at menu items.

### 4. $8,000 Budget (Tight but Doable)
- **85K United miles key:** May cover flights fully (~$1,200 cash value). Opens budget flexibility.
- **Accommodation strategy:** 3–4 star, central, not luxury. $220–280/night is reasonable in October (shoulder season).
- **Food allocation:** $2.5–3K for 10 days ($250–300/day) = $80–100 per person per day. Both love food, feasible with mix of casual + special dining.
- **Activities:** Temples $5–10 each, gardens $5–10. Japan affordable for sightseeing (not accommodation).

### 5. Depth Over Breadth (2–3 Cities, 1 Main Activity/Day)
- **Pace:** Unhurried, time for reflection, afternoon gardens, food exploration.
- **City selection:** 2 cities (Kyoto + Tokyo) best aligns with this — deep Kyoto temples/gardens, Tokyo food variety. 3 cities possible but busier.
- **Schedule structure:** Day 1 arrival + settle-in (light). Days 2–5 main activities. Day 6 travel. Days 7–9 main activities. Day 10 depart.
