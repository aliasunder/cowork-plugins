## Key Takeaways

### Kyoto Restaurant Findings
1. **Vegetarian opportunity:** Yudofu (Buddhist tofu hot pot) is authentically vegetarian and highly respected — not a workaround
2. **Michelin value:** One special dinner (Kikunoi or Hyotei, Oct 20) anchors the trip while accommodating Jordan's strict diet
3. **Booking lead time:** Michelin venues need 2–4 week advance booking; should book by early April for mid-October dates
4. **Tourist trap risk:** Geisha district sushi (Pontocho) and Ramen Alley offer convenience but inconsistent quality; avoid
