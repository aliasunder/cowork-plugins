# Kyoto Activities

## Overview
4 nights in Kyoto (Oct 18–21). Focused on temples and traditional experiences. Avoiding overly touristy spots.

## Day 1 — Oct 18 (Arrival)
- Afternoon arrival from Tokyo
- Check in to ryokan
- Evening: walk to nearby Nanzen-ji temple for sunset viewing

## Day 2 — Oct 19
- **Main:** Fushimi Inari shrine early morning (7am start to avoid crowds)
- Afternoon: Bamboo grove walk in Arashiyama
- Evening: explore local neighborhood

## Day 3 — Oct 20
- **Main:** Nanzen-ji temple complex + Silver Pavilion
- Afternoon: leisurely walk through Philosopher's Path
- Rain alternative: Nishiki Market food tour

## Day 4 — Oct 21 (Departure)
- Morning tea ceremony experience
- Afternoon: train to Osaka

## Accessibility Notes
- All temples have manageable stairs for Alex
- Early morning Fushimi Inari means fewer crowds, less cumulative strain
- Bamboo grove is flat
