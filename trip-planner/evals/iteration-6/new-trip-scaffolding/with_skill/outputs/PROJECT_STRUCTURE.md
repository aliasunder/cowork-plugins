**Travelers:**
- User: Vegetarian, food/temple enthusiast, balanced booking philosophy
- Alex: Omnivore, first-time Asia, language anxiety, old knee injury (manageable with planning)

**Budget:**
- Total: $8,000 USD for both travelers
- Points: 85,000 United miles
- Estimated breakdown: Flights $3-4K, Hotels $1.5-2K, Food $1.5-2K, Activities $400-500, Transport/misc $600-900

**Preferences:**
- Deep dive in 2-3 cities, not rushing
- Quality food focus (both travelers)
- Temples and gardens priority
- Mid-range to upscale hotels with character
- Refundable/flexible options valued for peace of mind
- Direct bookings preferred

**Constraints:**
- Alex's knee: Cumulative strain over long days + lots of stairs is limiting factor
- First-time Asia anxiety for both (language barrier for Alex especially)
- Plan for one main activity per day, rest breaks, flat terrain when possible
- Equal restaurant recommendations for vegetarian + omnivore
