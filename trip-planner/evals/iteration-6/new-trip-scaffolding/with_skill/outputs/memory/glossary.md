# Glossary

## Travel Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Shinkansen** | Japanese bullet train (high-speed rail) — fast, comfortable, central to inter-city travel in Japan |
| **JR Pass** | Japan Rail Pass — tourist pass covering most rail travel; may or may not be worth it depending on itinerary |
| **Suica/Pasmo** | IC cards for local transit in major cities (Tokyo, Kyoto, Osaka, etc.) — hassle-free for buses, trains, subway |
| **CPP** | Cents per point — metric for measuring points redemption value. Formula: (USD value ÷ points used) × 100. Example: 2,000 miles for a $30 flight = 1.5 CPP. |
| **Premium Cabin** | United business or first class |
| **Economy** | Standard seats on flights |
