## Tier 1: Must-Book (Michelin/Prestige)

### SHIGETSU — Oct 21, Lunch (Perfect Activity Co-Location)
- **Type:** Zen Buddhist shojin ryori (fully vegetarian)
- **Rating:** Michelin Bib Gourmand, 4.7/5
- **Address:** 68 Susukinobaba-cho, Saga-Tenryu-ji, Ukyo-ku, Kyoto 616-8385
- **Phone:** (075) 882-9725 (9am-5pm)
- **Hours:** 11:00 am – 2:00 pm daily (closed Thursdays)
- **Booking:** Minimum 3 days advance (Reserve by Oct 18)
- **Party Size:** 2+ guests
- **Cost:** ~$25-35 per person + ¥500 garden admission
- **Why:** Same-location as Day 4 Tenryu-ji Temple activity. Michelin-level experience. Jordan gets temple cuisine. Cultural pinnacle of trip.
- **Contact Strategy:** Call with these details:
  - "Shōjin ryōri for 2 people on October 21 at lunch"
  - Mention strict vegetarian diet for Jordan
  - Confirm seat availability and garden access

### AJIRO HONTEN — Oct 19, Lunch (Prestige Alternative)
- **Type:** Buddhist shojin ryori (certified vegan, no dairy)
- **Rating:** Michelin-recognized, 4.6/5, 60+ years, longest-running vegan Michelin star
- **Location:** Just south of Myōshin-ji Temple, Kita-ku
- **Hours:** Lunch 12pm-3pm | Dinner 5pm-9pm (enter by 7pm), closed Wednesdays
- **Booking:** Recommended for dinner; lunch walk-in possible
- **Cost:** Lunch ¥3,000 (~$20) | Dinner ¥6,000+ (~$40+)
- **Why:** Prestige option for Oct 19 (after Fushimi Inari 7am start). Different temple, same-level cuisine. Chef worked in temple kitchen.
- **Contact Strategy:** Email/phone for Oct 19 lunch reservation

## Tier 2: Flexible Backups (Walk-In OK, No Advance Booking)

### GION TANTO — Oct 18 & 20, Flexible (Vegan Okonomiyaki)
- **Type:** Traditional okonomiyaki (Japanese savory pancake), vegan menu available
- **Rating:** 4.5/5, known for long queues
- **Location:** Gion district (central Kyoto, near planned Day 2-3 activities)
- **Booking:** Walk-in only (no reservations)
- **Cost:** ~¥1,500-2,000 (~$12-15 per person)
- **Hours:** Open daily (confirm exact times)
- **Why:** Flexible, vegan-dedicated menu, cultural experience, convenient to activities
- **Queue Management:** Arrive 5-6pm or after 8pm to avoid peak; plan 30-45 min wait
- **Use As:** Primary for Oct 18 arrival dinner; secondary for Oct 20 Gion evening

### MUSUBI CAFE ARASHIYAMA — Oct 21, Light Lunch Alternative
- **Type:** Whole-grain vegetarian cafe
- **Rating:** 4.4/5
- **Location:** 3-min walk from Hankyu Arashiyama Station
- **Booking:** Walk-in acceptable
- **Cost:** ~¥1,200-1,500 (~$10-12 per person)
- **Menu:** Today's Special, chickpea curry, vegetable pasta, vegan options
- **Why:** Same-location day 4 activity, light meal option, full vegan accommodation
- **Use As:** Lunch alternative if Shigetsu unavailable (backup only)
