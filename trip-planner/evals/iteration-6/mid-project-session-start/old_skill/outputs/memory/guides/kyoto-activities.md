4 nights / 4 full days (Oct 18-21)

- Explore local neighborhood

- Evening in Gion

**Rain Alternative:** Nishiki Market food tour (covered market)

## Day 4: Oct 21
**Main Activity:** Arashiyama Bamboo Grove
- Afternoon visit (less crowded than morning)
- Tenryu-ji Temple nearby
- Evening stroll through neighborhood
