## Key Insights

### Budget Reality
- **$8,000 is generous** for 10 days + 85K miles
- Core spending: ~$3,500 (flights taxes, hotels, food, activities, transport)
- Buffer: ~$4,500 (splurges, emergencies, luxuries)
- **You can afford quality experiences** without financial stress

### Alex's Mobility (Not a blocker, just smart planning)
- Stairs compound fatigue after long days
- Solution: Morning temples (fresh), afternoon gardens (flat), evening dining (rested)
- Hotels with elevators + walkable neighborhoods essential
- Build in 1 rest day per city

### Food as Priority
- Your vegetarian + Alex's omnivore = both need quality options
- Frame restaurants as "here's what you both eat" not "vegetarian vs. omnivore"
- Japan food culture accommodates both well (vegetable ramen, soba, tofu specials)
- Budget ($500) allows nice dinners + casual meals

### First-Time Asia Support
- Language anxiety is real, solutions are practical (apps, phrase guides, pre-bookings)
- Transit is straightforward (IC card, clear signage, helpful staff)
- Cultural differences are expected, not stressful
- Deep travel (2–3 cities) prevents overwhelm
