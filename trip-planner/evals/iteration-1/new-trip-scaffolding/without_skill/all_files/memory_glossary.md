# Glossary — Japan Trip

**Purpose:** Quick reference for abbreviations, airport codes, terms, and Japanese cultural notes used in trip planning.

## Airport Codes
| Code | Airport | City | Notes |
|------|---------|------|-------|
| **NRT** | Narita International | Tokyo | ~60 km east of central Tokyo; international hub |
| **HND** | Haneda | Tokyo | Closer to city center; preferred for convenience |
| **KIX** | Kansai International | Osaka/Kyoto | Hub for western Japan; serves Kyoto (75 min), Osaka (50 min) |
| **ITM** | Osaka International (Itami) | Osaka | Domestic flights primarily; close to Osaka |

## Japan-Specific Terms
| Term | Meaning | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| **Ryokan** | Traditional Japanese inn | Usually includes meals; can be expensive but culturally rich |
| **Shojin ryori** | Buddhist temple cuisine | Vegetarian traditional cuisine; often excellent; reservations needed |
| **Onsen** | Hot spring bath | May be included in ryokan; check Alex's comfort level (public bathing) |
| **Tatami** | Woven floor mat | Rooms with tatami require removing shoes; may be uncomfortable for Alex's knee (floor sitting) |
| **Torii** | Gate to temple shrine | Usually at entrance; common landmark |
| **Zen garden** | Minimalist rock/sand garden | Often involves stairs initially, but can be viewed from pavilions |
| **Kaiseki** | Multi-course Japanese meal | Upscale, traditional; very aesthetic; often vegetarian-adaptable |
| **Tempura** | Battered & fried | Can be vegetable-focused for you |
| **Ramen** | Noodle soup | Usually has vegetarian broths (ask); quick meal |
| **Soba/Udon** | Buckwheat/wheat noodles | Good vegetarian options available |

## Travel Terminology
| Term | Meaning | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| **eUpgrade** | Electronic upgrade credit | Not applicable to Japan trip; Aeroplan/United rewards concept |
| **Round-trip** | Outbound + return flight | Your trip structure |
| **Award ticket** | Flight booked with miles | Applying to flights here (85K United miles) |
| **Points cpp** | Cents per point | Metric to evaluate miles value (higher = better deal) |
| **Domestic flight** | Flight within Japan | May be relevant if visiting 3+ distant cities (e.g., Tokyo → Kyoto) |
| **Rail pass (JR)** | Japan Rail Pass | Unlimited train travel; may or may not be cost-effective for 10 days |

## Travel Distance & Time
| Route | Distance | Time | Notes |
|-------|----------|------|-------|
| **Tokyo → Kyoto** | ~400 km | 2h 15m | Tokaido Shinkansen (bullet train); excellent connectivity |
| **Kyoto → Osaka** | ~75 km | 1h | Local train or bus; very convenient |
| **Osaka → Nara** | ~45 km | 45m | Day trip from Osaka; excellent temples |
| **Narita/Haneda → Tokyo center** | ~60/15 km | 60m/30m | Train or taxi; Haneda much closer |
| **Kansai (KIX) → Kyoto** | ~75 km | 75m | Haruka Express train to central Kyoto |

## Food & Dietary Notes
| Term | Notes |
|------|-------|
| **Vegetarian (yasai)** | Not a major cuisine category; you'll need to navigate menus or use apps |
| **No meat (niku nashi)** | Key phrase; useful for restaurants |
| **Tofu (doufu)** | Cheap, ubiquitous, versatile; good vegetarian base |
| **Vegetable tempura** | Reliable vegetarian dish |
| **Miso soup** | Usually vegetarian (verify dashi/broth); warm, comforting |
| **Omakase** | Chef's choice tasting (not suitable for vegetarians) |
| **Kaiseki (vegetarian)** | Traditional course meal; many high-end places offer veggie versions |
| **Buddhist temple meals** | Shojin ryori; 100% vegetarian; reservable at temple restaurants |

## Booking & Payment
| Term | Meaning | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| **Confirmation number** | Booking reference | Will get for flights, hotels, restaurants |
| **PNR** | Passenger Name Record (flights) | Required for check-in changes |
| **Cancellation policy** | Refund terms | Important for flexibility; "free cancellation 7+ days" preferred |
| **Non-refundable** | No $ back if cancelled | Avoid if possible |
| **Prepaid** | Paid at booking | Some hotels require full payment upfront |
| **Pay at property** | Settle at check-in | Preferred for flexibility |

## Accessibility Terms (For Alex's Needs)
| Term | Meaning | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| **Barrier-free** | Wheelchair accessible | Japan has good barrier-free infrastructure in major cities |
| **Elevator** | Mechanical lift | Essential for Alex; verify in hotel descriptions |
| **Ground floor/1st floor** | Minimal stairs to room | Preferred for Alex's knee |
| **Ramp** | Slope access | Alternative to stairs; common in temples |
| **Accessible route** | Stairs minimized | Some temples have alternate viewing areas |
| **Rest area** | Seating/break space | Parks, tea houses, convenience stores offer these |
