## Design Philosophy

### 1. Constraint-Aware Planning
- Identified 7 major constraints (budget, miles, mobility, dietary, experience, language, time)
- Each constraint addressed with practical solutions
- No constraint is treated as a blocker; all are manageable with smart planning

### 2. Deep Travel Model
- 2–3 cities max (not rushing)
- 3–5 nights per city (get to know place)
- Food + temples + gardens = natural daily rhythm
- Prevents "tourist checklist" exhaustion

### 3. First-Time Asia Support
- Language anxiety acknowledged and addressed
- Transit simplified (IC card, clear signage)
- Culture difference expected, not stressful
- Deep travel prevents overwhelm

### 4. Budget Realistic, Not Cheap
- Identified $4.5K buffer (not bare-bones)
- Food is priority (quality, not cost)
- Can do nice things (special dinners, ryokan night, guided tour)
- October peak season prices factored in

### 5. Cross-Session Continuity
- TASKS.md is single source of truth for blockers
- CLAUDE.md preserves decisions (no re-debating)
- On-disk files are searchable, versionable, persistent
- Session logs document progress + next steps
- Designed to scale across multiple planning sessions
