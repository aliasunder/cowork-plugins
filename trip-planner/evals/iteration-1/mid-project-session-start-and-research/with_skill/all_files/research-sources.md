## Key Data Points Extracted

**From Shigetsu (Tenryuji Temple):**
- Location: Arashiyama (pairs with Oct 20 activity day)
- Cuisine: Shojin ryori (100% vegan)
- Rating: ★★ Michelin
- Price: $120–180 per person
- Booking: Advance reservation essential (2–4 weeks, especially October)
- Notes: Seasonal, inside Tenryuji Temple compound

**From Daitokuji Ikkyu:**
- Location: Daitoku-ji Temple, central Kyoto
- Cuisine: Shojin ryori (100% vegan)
- Historical significance: 500+ years old, oldest existing shojin ryori restaurant in Kyoto
- Price: $80–120 per person
- Booking: Advance reservation recommended; walk-in possible
- Notes: Intimate, historic, quiet

**From Ajiro Honten:**
- Location: Near Myoshin-ji Temple
- Cuisine: Shojin ryori (100% vegan)
- Rating: ★ Michelin (longest-holding vegan Michelin star in Kyoto)
- Price: $90–140 per person
- Booking: Advance reservation required
- Notes: Multi-course Buddhist temple meals, sophisticated presentation
